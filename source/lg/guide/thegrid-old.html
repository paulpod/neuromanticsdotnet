The Grid

The new design uses a sixteen column grid, designed to fit well on a 1280 wide screen. This provides quite a lot of flexibility in layouts, allowing extra width for tables while still providing room to move the top level navigation into a vertical list on the left hand side (rather than fitting this into horizontal tabs).
