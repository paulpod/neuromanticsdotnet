Colours

Brand colours

Black and white. Use black and white to express clarity and openness.

The main brand colours are purple #763581 and green #9CC91C. Try to use these colours sparingly, and note the shades of green in the extended palette are based on this green for consistency.

Secondary colours

While a clean black and white approach is the aim, having a range of colours allows for linking, highlighting and layout with more flexibility.

These are chosen to complement the core brand colours - the blues for linking, the reds / ambers / yellows to alert and direct attention, the shades to use where complementary but neutral block areas are required, eg. instructions
